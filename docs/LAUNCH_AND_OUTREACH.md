# Launch And Outreach

Pilpul should be described plainly. The pitch is not growth, productivity, or AI. The pitch is sustained study with one other person.

## Short Description

Pilpul is an open-source web app for reading one text with one other person. Upload a PDF, find or invite a partner, enter a quiet study room, read together, and keep a shared notebook.

No feeds. No streaks. No ads. AI is silent unless summoned.

## Longer Description

Pilpul is a calm, open-source tool for sustained one-on-one study. It pairs people by text, pace, seriousness, language, and availability, then gives them a private room with a PDF reader, shared notes, voice or video, and optional bring-your-own-key AI.

The project is intentionally not a social network. It has no feed, followers, likes, badges, streaks, or ad tracking. Its success metric is not time in app. Its success metric is books finished and conversations deepened.

## Where To Share

- Book clubs and reading groups.
- Jewish learning and chavruta communities.
- Philosophy and classics communities.
- Indie web and small software communities.
- Open-source frontend and civic-tech spaces.
- Hacker News Show HN when the first complete flow is strong enough.

Avoid broad self-promotion before the first new-user path is dependable.

## Do Not Promise Yet

- That matching has enough users for every text.
- That every PDF import works.
- That AI can read every file from every provider.
- That the app has a moderation team.
- That mobile is finished.

## First Visibility Goal

Get 5 to 10 technically curious readers to try the full flow and open specific issues. Do not optimize for traffic before that.
